Vapi Widget Demo

This is a demo page for the Vapi AI voice assistant widget. Look for the widget in the bottom-right corner of the page.

How to use:

  • Click on the widget button in the bottom-right corner
  • Accept the terms and conditions if prompted
  • Click "Start" to begin a voice conversation
  • Speak with the AI assistant
  • Click "End Call" when you're done

Widget Features:

  • Voice mode enabled
  • Dark theme with teal accent color
  • Transcript display
  • Consent requirement
  • Positioned at bottom-right
